Small size

Say no to web bloat! Sitting at 2.8 KB gzipped JS it's probably one of the smallest color pickers out there.

Ease of use

Developed with mobile devices and keyboard usage in mind. Screen reader support.

Performant

Highly tuned touch interactions to stay above 30fps on lower end mobile devices.
